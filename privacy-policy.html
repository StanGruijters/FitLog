Privacy Policy

LOG. by Stan Gruijters — Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how LOG. ("the app", "we", "our") collects, uses and protects your personal data when you use our fitness tracking application.

1. Who we are

LOG. is a personal fitness tracking application developed by Stan Gruijters. You can contact us at: stan.gruijters@ifs.com

2. What data we collect

We collect only the data you actively provide:

  • Account data: email address and password (stored securely via Supabase Auth)
  • Profile data: display name, age, body weight and fitness goal (optional)
  • Fitness data: workouts, exercises, sets, reps, weights and personal records you log in the app
  • Routines: exercise routines you create and optionally share with other users

We do not collect location data, contacts, photos (unless you upload a profile picture), or any data from other apps on your device.

3. How we use your data

  • To provide the fitness tracking functionality of the app
  • To save your workouts and progress across devices
  • To enable routine sharing between users (only when you explicitly share)

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Data storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform. Data is stored in the EU (West Europe region). Supabase is SOC 2 compliant and encrypts data at rest and in transit.

Each user can only access their own data. Row-level security policies enforce this at the database level.

5. Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as you have an active account. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time via Settings → Account → Delete account in the app. Deletion is permanent and immediate.

6. Third-party services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — database and authentication. Privacy policy
  • Google Sign-In (optional) — if you choose to sign in with Google. Privacy policy

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data we hold about you (visible in the app)
  • Correct inaccurate data (via the app's Settings screen)
  • Delete your account and all data (via Settings → Delete account)
  • Export your data — contact us at the email below

8. Children

LOG. is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via the app. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last updated.

For any privacy-related questions or requests:
